Egghead.io

2014-08-09 :: Jeremy Bunting

If you’re checking out Google’s Angular, I highly recommend John Lindquist’s Egghead.io for learning the framework quickly and with lots of insight.

Angular is a super powerful framework and seems to be the way things are heading for building scalable large JS apps. If you’re building out a larger client side app, it’s worth a look.
